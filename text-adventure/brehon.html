You've chosen Brehon!

As the arbitrator of your community, you will face many difficult decisions. You are approached by an indignant farmer who claims that a chieftain has stolen from him. He says the chieftain agreed to pay 20 cows for a large swath of fine arable land, but when the chieftain saw the land, he only gave 16.

DO YOU:

Agree to look into the matter, walking over to the chieftain's house to hear his side of the story.

OR

Dismiss the farmer, explaining that the chieftain must have had a good reason and that four less cows is an inconsequential amount.
